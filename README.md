# NumbersSpiral
This program calculates sum of numbers placed on diagonals of clockwise squared numbers spiral.
  For spiral 5x5 (side size equal 5):
  **21** | 22 | 23 | 24 | **25**
  20 | **7** | 8 | **9** | 10
  19 | 6 | **1** | 2 | 11
  18 | **5** | 4 | **3** | 12
  **17** | 16 | 15 | 14 | **13**
  sum will be: 21 + 7 + 1 + 3 + 13 + 17 + 5 + 9 + 25 = 101
